Academic Issue Tracking System (AITS)

A comprehensive solution for logging, tracking, and resolving academic record-related issues at Makerere University. Improving transparency and efficiency for students, lecturers, and administrators.

S

For Students

Log academic issues, track status, receive notifications, and view issue history all in one place.

  • Missing Marks
  • Academic Appeals
  • Record Corrections
L

For Lecturers

View assigned issues, resolve problems, update statuses, and communicate with students.

  • Issue Resolution
  • Status Updates
  • Student Feedback
A

For Administrators

Manage all issues, assign to departments, monitor progress, and ensure timely resolution.

  • Issue Overview
  • Department Assignment
  • Progress Tracking
